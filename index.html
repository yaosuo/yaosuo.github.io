  • A power electronics engineer, researcher, and philosophic observer and visionary.
  • Currently work at Oak Ridge National Laboratory, USA Department of Energy, since 2015. Previously with Siemens Corporate Research for 7 years and TSDI, China Ministry of Railways (Now China Railway Design Corporation) for 9 years.
  • A graduate from University of New Brunswick in Canada and East China Jiaotong University.
  • Research focuses on multilevel converter topology, smart inverter's new control algorithms and functions, and modeling, analysis, and simulations of all power electronics grid.
  • Serve in editorial boards for IEEE TPEL, IEEE JESTPE, and IEEE OAJPE and technical program committees of IEEE ECCE, IEEE APEC, IEEE PEDG, and IEEE WiPDA for many years.
  • Notable achievements include leading the traction power-supply system R&D for China's first high-speed electric railway line currently commercially operated, and developing Siemens' first IEEE 1547 MW-scale solar PV inverter for North America marketplace.
  • 3 USA patents granted, 27 referred journal papers, and 80 referred conference papers.
